The existing Solution is Signify
Signify is a concept for a web plugin/extension that uses gesture and action recognition technology to help deaf and mute users communicate to other non-sign language users on video calling platforms
The extension allows the primary user to talk through sign language on video calls. The signs are detected and translated into a chosen language through subtitles and voice feedback. They have used Signify as an extension on Microsoft Teams.
In our application, the signs are detected and translated into text through subtitles, and voice feedback is also given to the user. The dataset we have used in our projects is of two types edge and contours which provides more accuracy and gives clearer output.
